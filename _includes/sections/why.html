Why Use Amalgam8?

Amalgam8 simplifies microservice integration tasks and enables you to focus on the DevOps functions such as canary testing, A/B testing, red/black deployments, and systematic resilience testing.

You can use the Amalgam8 platform with popular container runtimes like Docker, Kubernetes, Marathon/ Mesos, or your favorite cloud provider such as IBM Bluemix, Google Cloud Platform, or Amazon AWS.
